Natural Language Processing ( NLP)

This project explores the representation of different languages in NLP systems. It studies the huge gap between the representation of languages such as English and underrepresented languages like Farsi, Urdu, Wolof. Being a native Wolof speaker, I contribute to the Wolof portion of the project. Wolof is spoken in some parts of West Africa with a modest population of about 10 million speakers.

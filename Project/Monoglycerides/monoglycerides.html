Monoglycerides

As a research assistant in the Chemistry department at Iona College, I was tasked with running the energy calculations of monoglyceride membranes. The membranes were generated using CHARMM-GUI. Specific target membranes were later generated using python scripts  (figure below). NAMD was used for the molecular dynamics simulation, while   VMD was used for visualization. Result assembly was done using   GROMACS. A summary of the methodology, results, and conclusions is presented in the figure below.
